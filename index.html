KubeMate

ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE BASED CENTRALIZED RESOURCE MANAGEMENT APPLICATION FOR DISTRIBUTED SYSTEMS

What is KubeMate?

KubeMate is a centralized resource management tool to optimize deployment of resources, that also provides a low latency container management system

Our Domain

 The apparent need for improved microservice governance modeling strategies, along with some of the prevalent issues in current microservice governance methodologies, have been highlighted in several publications throughout the years. In this regard, a key focus of such publications has been the need for holistic modeling strategies that capture recent advances in deployment technology as well as addressing issues pertaining to the inability of monitoring frameworks to effectively capture microservice performance metrics.

 The need for new modeling strategies that facilitate the creation of holistic microservice governance policies through the incorporation of effective and efficient use of monitoring solutions.

  Can the current container management system reduce the network overhead added to the Kubernetes cluster?

  How monitor and structure pod-level and app-level matrics to determine optimal deployment strategy?

  How effective is the proposed optimization model compared to current optimization models?

  How to maintain resiliency in optimal microservice deployment?

  Develop an automated container management system inside the Kubernetes cluster to reduce the network overhead.

  Enable the Service Mesh on the deployed Kubernetes cluster and gather app-level and pod-level matrics

  Implement a model that predicts the optimal deployment strategy based on the data gathered by the Service Mesh

  Develop a monitoring system that logs all the Kubernetes cluster information and maintains the resiliency of the microservices.
